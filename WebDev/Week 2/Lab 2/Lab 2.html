Table #1 - Video Games.
Title Summary Rating
Tom Clancy's: Rainbow Six Seige

First person shooter based on the Tom Clancy novels. The game has been around since 2015 and still has a large following. With new content dropping every couple months as well as challenges to keep players hooked it is no wonder the game still has a cult following.

I would say the downfall to the game being around this long is it's "hackability". Meaning that there have been many ways to cheat in the game making it unfair for players that enjoy the game as it is.

 4 out of 5 Stars
Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2019

A reboot of the classic Call of Duty 4 Modern Warfare from 2009. The campaign, while it was rather short, packed an impressive story. Reintroducing the characters from the original trilogy following a new story from a similar timeline.

The multiplayer left a lot to be desired. Not many of the maps were very playable or balanced. The spawn system did not help in that fact. In the end most player only ended up playing a few of the game mode options and on certain maps.

At the time, battle royale games were on the rise, Call of Duty was not going to miss out. They introduced Warzone, their take on the game mode. It has taken of and become one of the top battle royale games.

 3 out of 5 Stars
Rocket League

The, now, free to play car soccer game is a fan favorite. Taking in aspects from a lot of different video game styles they are able to give a diverse experience, not to mention rake in the cash.

Offering several different game modes, as well as a competitive game mode, there is something for every player, even those that are not a fan of soccer (myself included).

Can't forget to mention the seasonal and time limited game modes that are only around MAYBE once a year.

4 out of 5 Stars
Portal 2

The second iteration of the class "Portal" game improves on the original by adding more features and challenges.

The puzzle game challenges players to utilize the game mechanics to make your way through, at times, tricky puzzles.

Even though I am mainly a fan of first person shooters, this game was A LOT of fun to play through!

 4 out of 5 Stars
